About: I am a Senior Researcher (Economist and Data Scientist) at Mathematica with extensive experience in causal inference and advanced statistical modeling. I design experiments, analyze complex datasets, communicate findings to broad audiences, and lead teams. Much of my research focuses on approaches to measuring a broad set of skills (e.g., perseverance and emotional control) and evaluating interventions designed to improve them.
